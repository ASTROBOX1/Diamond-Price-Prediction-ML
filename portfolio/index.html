🧠 Predictive Healthcare Analytics Platform

AI-powered clinical decision support that identifies at-risk patients before it's too late

⚠ The Business Problem

Stroke is the 2nd leading cause of death globally. Clinical staff make reactive decisions — intervening only after symptoms appear. The cost of a late diagnosis: higher mortality, longer ICU stays, millions in preventable long-term care.

🔬 The Solution

A multi-model analytics platform using XGBoost + SMOTE to solve severe class imbalance (4.9% positive class). Deployed as a 6-page Streamlit dashboard, it gives clinicians a real-time risk score per patient — with explainability so they know why the patient is flagged.
